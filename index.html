About the Conference

The 1st ICPACOIT 2025 discusses about the various aspects of advanced communications systems and networking, including cloud and virtualisation solutions, management technologies, and vertical application areas. It brings together the cutting-edge researchers from all over the world to present the latest and upcoming technologies and also provides an excellent international forum for sharing knowledge and results in theory, methodology and applications in Networking and Communications. The recent developments of parallel computing in various fields of application by using machine learning techniques and it intelligence to handling the huge volume of data to bring the solution to the advanced technologies in faster manner. Furthermore, the newer and innovative ideas will be well groomed with adequate technical support and the core competent technical domains in Communication to improve the intellectual aspects. The conference looks for significant contributions towards wired and wireless networks in theoretical and practical aspects. It also provides a premier platform for Scientists, Researchers, Practitioners and Academicians to present and discuss about the most recent innovations, trends and concerns as well as practical challenges encountered in this field. We invite papers tackling various issues in parallel computing and the solutions provide to them through machine learning.





We solicit research and experience papers as well as research-in-progress and practitioner reports in any of the technical areas listed under Scope & Topics.



Track 1: Parallel computing in Communication
Track 2: Machine Learning Intelligence for Parallel computing
Track 3: Parallel Computing for Software Services
